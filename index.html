Our love story

First Date

April 16, 2010

Matt and Casey met in high school AP Physics class. Matt sat next to Casey and started talking with her. They started working on projects together and hanging out after class. Eventually Matt asked Casey out and they had their first date eating frozen yogurt at Menchies.

It's Official

July 30, 2010

After a successful first date and a summer full of many more dates, Matt asked Casey to be his girlfriend.

Off To College

September 2011 - June 2015

Once they graduated high school, Matt and Casey went off to college. They spent the next 4 years traveling back and forth between San Luis Obispo and Santa Barbara. Even though there was distance between them, they grew closer than ever.

First Apartment

July 2015

Two degrees and two job offers later, Matt and Casey packed up their car and headed to San Francisco to move into their first apartment together.

Proposal

Dec 5, 2019

A surprise trip to Paris turned into the most magical proposal. Matt took Casey to Paris for her birthday. On the way to what she thought was a fancy birthday dinner, they came across some photographers asking for photos. Matt was actually working with the photographers to set the stage for his proposal. He asked her to marry him and she said the easiest yes of her life.

FAQ

Are there any COVID-19 safety protocols?
In order to keep our guests healthy, we request that everyone in attendance is either fully vaccinated against COVID, or is able to show us a negative COVID test if they are not vaccinated. We will continue to update this answer if any venue requirements change.
What shoud I wear?
The attire is black-tie optional, so we would like our guests to dress up. Tuxedos are not required, but you are welcome to wear one. Dresses ranging from cocktail attire to floor length gowns are also welcome.
Can I bring a guest or children
